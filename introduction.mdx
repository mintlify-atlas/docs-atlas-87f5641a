## What is Alliance IGAD Innovation Hub?

Alliance IGAD Innovation Hub is an AI-powered SaaS platform designed to streamline and enhance the proposal writing process for development organizations, NGOs, and research institutions. By leveraging AWS Bedrock with Claude AI, the platform guides users through a structured workflow that transforms Request for Proposals (RFPs) and initial concepts into comprehensive, donor-aligned proposal documents.

<Note>
  The platform is specifically designed for development organizations working in the IGAD (Intergovernmental Authority on Development) region, helping teams respond faster to funding opportunities while maintaining high quality standards.
</Note>

## How It Works

The platform guides you through a 4-step workflow:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Information Consolidation">
    Upload your RFP document, initial concept notes, reference proposals, and existing work documents. The platform automatically analyzes and extracts key information.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Concept Review & Generation">
    Review AI-generated insights about your concept's alignment with the RFP. Select sections needing elaboration and generate enhanced concept documents.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Structure & Workplan">
    Define your proposal structure, timeline, and work packages. The AI helps you create detailed workplans aligned with donor requirements.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Proposal Review & Export">
    Review the complete proposal, make final edits, and export to Word or PDF format for submission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Analysis

Built on **AWS Bedrock with Claude Sonnet 4**, the platform provides:

- **Contextual Understanding**: Analyzes RFP requirements in context, identifying implicit expectations beyond explicit criteria
- **Gap Analysis**: Compares your concept against RFP requirements to identify missing elements
- **Donor Alignment**: Ensures generated content matches donor priorities and evaluation criteria
- **Citation Support**: References your uploaded documents when generating content

### Document Management

Robust document handling with:

- **Multi-format Support**: Upload PDF, DOCX, and TXT files
- **S3 Storage**: Secure, versioned document storage on AWS S3
- **Vector Search**: Automatic vectorization of reference documents for semantic search
- **Text Extraction**: Intelligent text extraction from uploaded documents

### Collaborative Workflow

<Note>
  The platform supports collaborative proposal development with:
  - User comments on AI suggestions
  - Section-by-section review and approval
  - Revision tracking
  - Export to standard formats for team review
</Note>

## Use Cases

### Development Organizations

Respond to donor RFPs faster by automating document analysis and first-draft generation. Focus your team's time on strategy and refinement rather than formatting and structure.

### Research Institutions

Leverage past successful proposals to inform new submissions. The platform learns from your reference documents to maintain institutional knowledge and writing style.

### Grant Writers

Manage multiple proposal pipelines efficiently. The platform tracks progress across proposals and ensures consistency in quality and compliance.

## Security & Compliance

- **Authentication**: AWS Cognito for secure user management
- **Authorization**: Role-based access control (RBAC) for admin and user roles
- **Data Privacy**: All documents stored in private S3 buckets with encryption
- **Audit Trail**: Complete activity tracking in DynamoDB

<Warning>
  The platform handles sensitive proposal documents and organizational information. Ensure you follow your organization's data governance policies when uploading documents.
</Warning>
